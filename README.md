          <h2>Step-by-step: How to cut a low fade</h2>
          <ol>
            <li>Start with clean, dry hair. Comb and section the top.</li>
            <li>Using clippers with guard #2, cut the area below the parietal ridge.</li>
            <li>Switch to guard #1.5 and blend upward using an upward flicking motion.</li>
            <li>Use a clipper-over-comb or blending guard to soften the transition to the longer top.
            </li>
            <li>Detail the edges around the ears and nape with a trimmer.</li>
            <li>Finish by texturizing the top with scissors and styling product.</li>
